About Us



PT. PAHAE MUARA ENERGI is a supplier and services company recognized for supply product for well completion in the global oil and gas industry in Indonesia. Our company is located in Jakarta (DKI Jakarta) well known as a capital city of Indonesia.

Founded in 2017, the company has focused on supply and services for the oil and gas industry by meeting client needs for downhole completion tools. Our mission is to accomplish client projects to perform their business in the most efficient and profitable way.

Our focus in on quality, schedule and value for client.

Health, Safety and Environment Policy Statement

PT. PAHAE MUARA ENERGI is a supplier for well completion tools / downhole tools for Oil and Gas Industry, and committed to provide high quality products to meet with customers satisfaction. The company is committed to meet with the requirements of the integrated HSE Management System effectively and supported by the entire management and employees.

Company purpose and target to implemented K3 Management System and OHSAS 18001 in work area entangled by Management, Employees, situation and work environment which integrated to prevent and reduce of accident, fire and disease effect of job, and also safe work, efficient & productive.

To control the impact of environmental pollution in vicinity of the company's activities. Comply with all laws and regulations in the field of K3 and Environment regulations.

To meet this objective the company needs a joint, understandable communication and coordinated effort among the entire management, employees, customers and visitors. Evaluations will be periodically scheduled whenever this is appropriately and effectively Implemented as guided by the company HSE Management System.

Jakarta, 03 JANUARI 2020
